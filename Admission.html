    MASTER DEGREE APPLICATION

    Applications must be submitted online at http://mohon.ums.edu.my/pasca/ Applications for research programmes are open throughout the year. Candidate may apply anytime except for programmes with pre-requisite courses. Applications for Research programmes are open throughout the year.

    Application Guidelines

    Step 1

    Kindly complete on-line application form at http://mohon.ums.edu.my/pasca/

    Step 2

    Upload all required document as needed

    Step 3

    1. Once all the online procedure is completed, kindly go to “Click To Confirm Application” button to get the complete application form. Please keep the documents for your own record.
    2. You are not allowed to edit your on-line application after pressing “Click To Confirm Application” button.
    3. You may check your application status via on-line from http://mohon.ums.edu.my/pasca/ two months after the date of your application.
